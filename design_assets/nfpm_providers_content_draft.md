2. **Specialized Nutritional Deficiency Therapy**: Personalized vitamin and mineral hydration therapy designed to support nerve healing and reset pain signaling pathways.
3. **Patient Education & Lifestyle Counseling**: Robust, personalized counseling to support long-term outcomes and sustainable relief.
