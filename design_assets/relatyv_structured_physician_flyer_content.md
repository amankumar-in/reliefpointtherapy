## Neurofunctional Pain Management (NFPM)
NFPM Therapy is built on three foundational pillars:

### 1. High Pulse Electroanalgesia Therapy
The highest FDA-cleared high pulse electroanalgesia therapy available in the country. This therapy is not available through general practitioners, chiropractors, or standard pain clinics.

### 2. Specialized Nutritional Deficiency Therapy
Personalized vitamin and mineral hydration therapy designed to support nerve healing and reset pain signaling pathways.

### 3. Patient Education and Lifestyle Counseling
A robust and personalized education and lifestyle counseling regimen to support long-term outcomes and sustainable pain relief.
