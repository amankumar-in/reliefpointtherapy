# **NFPM Provider Referral & Clinical Information**

Neurofunctional Pain Management (NFPM) offers a non-invasive, drug-free option for patients with chronic pain, mobility limitations, or nerve-related symptoms who need alternatives to medications, injections, or procedures.

Our goal is to be a trusted clinical resource and long-term partner for providers seeking additional tools to support their patients.

## **Clinical Overview of NFPM**

Neurofunctional Pain Management (NFPM) is an FDA-cleared, non-invasive neuromodulation therapy that uses high-pulse electrical currents to influence pain signaling, circulation, inflammation, and tissue recovery.

NFPM is designed to:

* Reduce peripheral and central pain signaling

* Improve microcirculation and oxygen delivery

* Support neuromuscular retraining

* Accelerate wound healing in select cases

* Improve functional mobility and quality of life

Treatments are delivered in-home by a registered nurse and follow a structured, evidence-based protocol.

---

## **Technology Used**

### **Electroanalgesia vs Conventional Modalities**

NFPM utilizes FDA-cleared electroanalgesia devices designed for neuromodulation and chronic pain conditions.

Key distinctions from traditional modalities:

* Higher pulse frequencies than standard TENS

* Deeper tissue penetration

* Designed specifically for chronic and neuropathic pain

* Not a consumer-grade or wellness device

This technology targets pain at the nervous system level, rather than masking symptoms.

*FDA Certificate available upon request.*
